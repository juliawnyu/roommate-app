Welcome to Othmer Hall!

Located in Downtown Brooklyn, the Donald F. and Mildred Topp Othmer Residence Hall is an 18-story building, housing over 400 NYU students in both traditional and apartment-style suites. The residence includes student lounge access on each floor and laundry facilities in the basement. Our 12 Resident Assistants (RAs) and one Faculty Fellow-in-Residence are here to help facilitate community building, enhance your academic experience, plan events, and provide support during your time at Othmer Hall. Throughout the year, there are also many opportunities to get involved, especially with our Hall Council - our very own hall government where you can run for an elected position or serve on the general assembly!

Getting Around

Subway Access: - 2 blocks to Jay Street-Metro Tech Station for A, C, F, R trains. - 5 blocks to Borough Hall Station for 2, 3, 4, 5 trains. - 7 blocks to DeKalb Ave Station for B, Q, R trains. The closest dining hall is Jasper Kane Dining Center, which is located in Rogers Hall across Johnson Street. Please note: all Othmer residents are required to maintain a minimum meal plan.
